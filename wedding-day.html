Wedding Day Details

Ceremony & Reception

Both the ceremony and reception will be held at Bottom Lounge on Friday, September 22, 2017.

Getting to Bottom Lounge

We encourage the following methods of transportation:
CTA (take a bus or the Green Line (train))
Uber/Lyft
Divvy bike (there’s a station at Ashland & Lake - go for it!)
If you’re driving, Bottom Lounge has a parking lot & there’s also street parking.

How do I dress?

Dress like you’re going to a wedding that’s in a bar/restaurant/music venue, because that’s where you’ll be on Friday, September 22, 2017. It’s a wedding, look nice. No denim & no cargo shorts.

Unplugged Ceremony
We will be having a unplugged ceremony. We want everyone to be fully present and view the ceremony through their real eyes, not a camera or smartphone.
