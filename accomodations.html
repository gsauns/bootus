Accomodations

Getting to Chicago

From O'Hare Airport (ORD):
The Blue Line (train) takes you into the Loop (where you’ll likely be staying).
More information on taking the CTA from the airport into the Loop. We highly recommend picking up a Ventra card/CTA pass at the airport to ride public trans all weekend.
From Midway Airport (MDW):
The Orange Line takes you into the Loop.
More information on taking the CTA from the airport into the Loop. We highly recommend picking up a Ventra card/CTA pass at the airport to ride public trans all weekend.
Don’t rent a car. Embrace public transportation or Uber/Lyft. Chicago is a wonderful walking city and we have an easy-to-navigate grid system.
Where to Stay:
Hotel blocks are currently in the works. However, we want friends and family to be comfortable picking their accommodations within their price range. We highly recommend Airbnb -- think about staying with friends or family in the same house or apartment! As long as you're within 3 miles of the city center (the Loop), you'll be fine.
More information on taking the CTA from the airport into the Loop. We highly recommend picking up a Ventra card/CTA pass at the airport to ride public trans all weekend.
Neighborhood suggestions (for Airbnb or alternate hotels): Ukrainian Village/West Town (our neighborhood), Wicker Park, Logan Square, West Loop (where the venue is), Lincoln Park, River North, Near North, Old Town or the Loop (downtown). Take a look at our map to get a sense of where you’ll be all weekend.
