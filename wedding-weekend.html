Wedding Weekend

Thursday, September 21st

  • 8:00 pm - we'd like to invite guests that have arrived to join us at the Copper Fox Gastropub (inside the Courtyard Marriott hotel) for a welcome drink. Hosted bar until 9:00 pm.

Friday, September 22nd | Wedding Day!

Both the ceremony and reception will be held at Bottom Lounge.
  • 5:30 pm- guests can arrive at Bottom Lounge (upstairs) to grab a beverage
  • 6:00 pm- ceremony starts
  • 6:30 pm- cocktail hour
  • Dinner and dancing to follow
We will be having an unplugged ceremony. We want everyone to be fully present and view the ceremony through their real eyes, not a camera or smartphone.
Bottom Lounge
1375 W Lake St.
Chicago, IL 60607

Getting to Bottom Lounge

We encourage the following methods of transportation:
  • CTA
    • The 9 and 20 buses have stops within a short walk.
    • The Green Line train's Ashland station is a short walk as well.
  • Uber/Lyft
  • Divvy bike (there’s a station at Ashland & Lake - go for it!)
  • If you’re driving, Bottom Lounge has a parking lot, and there’s also street parking.

Saturday, September 23rd (optional)

  • 3:00 pm- Goose Island 312 Urban Block Party
The bride and groom will be at Goose Island Brewery (Fulton & Wood - NOT the one on Clybourn!) at 3pm. We'll be donating to one of the charities that Goose Island is partnering with so your admission to the fest will be free! Please see anyone in the wedding party so you can get a wristband to get into the festival.
Official statement from Goose about children at the block party: "Children are not encouraged as it's a beer event designed for adults 21+, but technically the little ones are allowed."
A bit more info for those who have no idea what this is all about: Goose Island's annual street festival outside of its Fulton Street production facility returns for two days of music accompanied by a copious amount of locally-brewed beer. You can expect some food stalls, some special timed releases of Bourbon County Stout, and a lot of hanging out with all our friends and family.
